Gallery 5 is in the picturesque crofting village of Tolsta Chaolais on the Hebridean island of Lewis. Converted from what remained of an old blackhouse, a stone built house of the highlands and islands with a thatched roof, it is now a unique light-filled studio gallery, a great space for both artist and visitors.

The location is stunning with views across Loch Roag to Bernera and the Uig hills, a source of endless inspiration. It's here that Margaret Stevenson, a graduate of Glasgow School of Art, paints in oils and watercolour capturing the mood of the islands, its forms and life.
